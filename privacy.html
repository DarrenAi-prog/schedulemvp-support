Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-02

ScheduleMVP (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app may process and how it is used.

Information We Process

  • Account information (such as email and name) used to create and manage your account.
  • Work data you or your organization enters (schedules, roles, time-off requests, messages, shift swaps).
  • Basic diagnostics to help us troubleshoot issues (e.g., error logs). We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data.

How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality (scheduling, requests, messaging, swaps).
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse.
  • To provide customer support.

Sharing

Your data is shared within your organization as part of the app’s normal operation. We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account/organization remains active, or as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.
